25, a year. as the clock winds down, i realized: growth is a lot of things to a lot of people. mine was nothing like you have ever heard of.
the year kicked off with a thud.
i lost my job.
it killed my stability. the one thing that carried my plans was nowhere to be found.
i tried to force the crypto thing. perp dexes? the worst. i couldn’t ‘yap’ about projects i had no interest in. it felt alien.
i picked up cloud engineering. i ground through a semester, hoping to build the next big thing. but my mind was wandering. you can’t force the flow when your head is not in it.
then, the vandals hit.
lost a farm. all the money. herdsmen. kidnapping.
20 million naira ransom or the wreckage.
i chose life.
but this is still a year of growth. one valid lesson:
you can just do things.
starting a new farm. learning to code. fighting with gemini. i found out i care about how the environment should be safe for people. 2025 tested every limit, but i am ending it stronger.
if you have read this far, i love you so much. i’ll see you in 2026.
